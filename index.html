About Me!

Hey! I'm Scott Sikorski, a Masters student in computer science at the University of Virginia. I recently graduated from the University of Rochester in 2023 where I previously worked in parallel computing with GPUs using CUDA and OpenMP and compiler optimizations with software analysis techniques.


I have primarily done industry work in Embedded Software for consumer electronics. This includes wearable fitness devices to help people track their workouts and train more effectively in their respective sports. Being a big runner myself, I'm very passionate in creating products that can help people train to reach their goals and dreams. "No Human is Limited" ~ Eluid Kipchoge (Former Marathon World Record Holder)


My current research interests are in learning for resource constrained environments, primarily robots. I have worked to include Lifelong Learning (Minimal Forgetting of Previous Tasks) in completeing Tasks in multiple environments.
